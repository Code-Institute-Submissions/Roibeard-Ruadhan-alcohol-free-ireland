A place to add your experience with Alcohol Free Drinks

A space to share a variety of storys from drink reviews, dry January, to times of soberiety. 20% of the population of Ireland do not drink alcohol & often feel left out of weekend/social gatherings.

Have you enjoyed any non alcohol drinks recently that you would like to share your experience of, feel free to share in the blog space. Walking in to large supermarkets recently I can't help notice how the non alcoholic section continues to grow, take you more shelf space & seems to add a new drink every week.

Many people are quietly considerning taking a break from alcohol & would like to learn more about other peoples experiences with this. The Blog provides that space for people to share there good & bad experiences with abstaining from alcohol.

Becoming More Widespread
